Common Questions

How long does it take to build a custom solution? +

Most projects take 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. We start with a discovery session to understand your needs, then move quickly to implementation. Simple websites can be ready in 2-3 weeks, while complex AI automation systems typically take 4-6 weeks. We'll give you an accurate timeline during our discovery call.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you? +

Absolutely not. We translate tech-speak into plain English and handle all the technical complexity. You tell us what problems you're trying to solve, and we'll build the solution. We also provide training so you can manage day-to-day operations without needing a developer.

What if I need changes or updates later? +

We build systems that are easy to update and scale. For ongoing changes, we offer flexible month-to-month support packages. No long-term contracts required. Many clients handle small updates themselves using the training we provide, while we're available for bigger changes or new features.

How much does a typical project cost? +

Every business is different, so we create custom quotes based on your specific needs. Professional websites typically start around $3,000-$5,000. AI automation projects range from $5,000-$15,000 depending on complexity. We're upfront about costsno hidden fees or surprise charges. Book a free discovery call to get an accurate quote for your project.

What industries do you work with? +

We work with small to medium-sized businesses across many industrieslaw firms, financial advisors, HVAC companies, consultants, and more. If you're a business that wants to use technology to work smarter (not harder), we can help. Our solutions are custom-built, so they work for your specific industry and business model.
